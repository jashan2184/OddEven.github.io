Cities statistics[edit]

As per 2011 Census of India, Towns with population of 100,000 and above are called Cities.[1]

Visakhapatnam is the most populated city with a population of 2,035,922 after merger of surrounding towns of Gajuwaka, Anakapalle, Gopalapatnam, Bheemunipatnam Municipalities into Visakhapatnam Municipal Corporation (VMC) and it was upgraded to Greater Visakhapatnam Municipal Corporation (GVMC). Srikakulam is the least populated Municipal Corporation with a population of 147,015. Proposals to upgrade Vijayawada, Kakinada and Rajamahendravaram into Greater Municipal Corporations are under consideration for long time[2][3][4].

Visakhapatnam, Kakinada, Tirupati and Amaravati are being developed as Smart cities by Government of India under Smart Cities Mission[5][6]

The cities written in bold are the headquarters of their respective district.
