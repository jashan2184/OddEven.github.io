Odd Even

This app is built to make Odd Even scheme more easier and to spread this scheme all over the country.

(FOUNDER) Jashanpreet Singh

What is Odd Even?

Odd Even is a scheme which is started by the government.Under this scheme every shop is given a number and according to that number one day even number shops will open and the another day odd number shops will open.The work of this app is to help the people and the government and to make this scheme more easier.

How it Works?

step 1:register your name,phone number,shop's full address,pincode

step 2:After registration we will check your address

step 3:we will send your's shop number to you

step 4:everyday you will get a message/e-mail that you have to open your shop or not
